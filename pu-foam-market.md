<p><p>The PU foam market is categorized into Type I and Type II based on their density and application. Type II foam, or high-density foam, has a density range of 1.8 to 3.0 lb/ft³, providing superior durability and support, making it ideal for industrial uses, automotive seating, and construction insulation.</p></p>
