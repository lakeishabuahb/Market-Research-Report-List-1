<p><p>The Automatic Visual Inspection (AVI) System market comprises two primary types: front inspection and back inspection. Front inspection focuses on evaluating the visible surfaces of products, ensuring quality by detecting defects or anomalies before packaging. In contrast, back inspection examines the underside or internal components of items, emphasizing safety and compliance by identifying potential issues that may not be visible externally. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing product reliability and optimizing manufacturing processes across various industries.</p></p>
