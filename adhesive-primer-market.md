Titanate primers offer strong adhesion and durability, especially in challenging environments. Zirconate primers provide excellent moisture resistance and enhanced bonding on diverse substrates, making them valuable in specialized applications. Each type serves unique applications based on surface compatibility and desired performance.</p></p>
