<p><p>Copper barrier CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for metal removal. There are two primary types: non-aqueous copper barrier slurries and aqueous copper barrier slurries. Non-aqueous slurries typically utilize organic solvents, enhancing the removal rate and reducing the risk of corrosion. Aqueous slurries, on the other hand, use water-based solutions, offering better stability and environmental safety. Both types are vital for achieving optimal surface smoothness and planarity during the fabrication of microelectronic devices.</p></p>
