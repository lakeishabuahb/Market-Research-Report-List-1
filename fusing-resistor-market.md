<p><strong>The Fusing Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resettable Fuse Resistor</li><li>One-time Fuse Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fusing resistor market comprises two main types: resettable fuse resistors and one-time fuse resistors. Resettable fuse resistors can endure overcurrent conditions and automatically reset after cooling down, making them ideal for applications where repeated protection is necessary. One-time fuse resistors, on the other hand, provide a one-time protective solution, permanently disconnecting the circuit upon exceeding specified limits. Each type serves distinct needs in electrical and electronic applications, balancing safety and functionality based on operational requirements.</p></p>
