<p><p>The porous metal market is categorized based on porosity levels, which significantly influence their applications and properties. Low porosity materials (below 30%) are typically dense and used in structural applications requiring strength and durability. Medium porosity metals (30-60%) offer a balance, making them suitable for filtration and lightweight components. High porosity materials (above 60%) possess extensive voids, ideal for applications needing lightweight structures or enhanced fluid permeability, such as in biomedical or aerospace fields. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
