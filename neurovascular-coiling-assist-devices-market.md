<p><p>Neurovascular coiling assist devices are used in the treatment of intracranial aneurysms, enhancing the effectiveness of endovascular coil embolization. The market primarily includes coil assist stents and coil assist balloons. Coil assist stents provide a scaffold to support coils within the aneurysm, improving stability and preventing re-bleeding. Coil assist balloons, on the other hand, temporarily occlude blood flow, allowing for optimal coil placement. Both types improve procedural outcomes and patient safety in neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
