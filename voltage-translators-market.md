<p><p>The voltage translators market is segmented into three main types: unidirectional, bidirectional, and direction-controlled voltage translators. Unidirectional voltage translators facilitate signal transmission in one direction, making them suitable for specific applications where signal flow needs to be controlled. Bidirectional voltage translators allow signals to move in both directions, offering flexibility for interconnecting different devices. Direction-controlled voltage translators provide the ability to set the direction of signal flow based on external control signals, enabling optimized communication in complex systems.</p></p>
