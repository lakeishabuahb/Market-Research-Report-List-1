<p><ul><li>Spay Through Overcaps</li><li>Traditional "Dust-Cover" Overcaps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerosol overcap market is primarily divided into two types: spray through overcaps and traditional "dust-cover" overcaps. Spray through overcaps are designed to allow the spray nozzle to be easily accessed, enabling efficient delivery of the product while preventing contamination. In contrast, traditional dust-cover overcaps serve as protective covers that prevent dust and debris from entering the aerosol can but do not facilitate spraying. Each type caters to specific consumer needs and enhances product functionality and hygiene.</p></p>
