<p><p>The automotive fuel nozzle market comprises several types, including centrifugal nozzles, evaporator tube nozzles, and oil throwing nozzles. Centrifugal nozzles utilize rotational force to atomize fuel, enhancing combustion efficiency. Evaporator tube nozzles facilitate the evaporation of fuel, improving throttle response and emissions control. Oil throwing nozzles disperse lubricant oil in engine systems to enhance performance and reduce wear. Other types may include specialized nozzles for niche applications, catering to various engine designs and performance requirements in the automotive sector.</p></p>
