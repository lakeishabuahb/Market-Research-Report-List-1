<p><p>The operating otoscopes market consists of two main types: halogen and xenon otoscopes. Halogen operating otoscopes utilize halogen bulbs, offering a warm light ideal for detailed examination, while providing durability and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, xenon operating otoscopes use xenon lamps, which deliver brighter, more intense illumination for enhanced visibility during procedures. Both types cater to healthcare professionals, particularly in ENT and surgical settings, enabling precise diagnostics and interventions through superior optical performance and illumination characteristics.</p></p>
