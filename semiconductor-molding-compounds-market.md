<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Granular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor molding compounds market can be categorized into two primary types: liquid and granular. Liquid molding compounds offer high fluidity and are ideal for complex geometries, ensuring uniform filling and minimizing voids during the molding process. They are often used in applications requiring intricate designs and tight tolerances. In contrast, granular molding compounds consist of pre-formed pellets that provide ease of handling and storage. These are typically used in standard molding processes, offering flexibility and efficiency in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
